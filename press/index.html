Boilerplate

Short

Hypercreative trains the teams of the world’s most demanding companies to create useful ideas on demand, under pressure. It turns creativity from a personality trait into a trainable discipline.

Long

Hypercreative is a corporate creativity training company. It teaches leadership teams and their people to generate useful, original ideas on demand, under real pressure and on a deadline, through keynotes and tailor-made programmes. The method was built inside high-pressure creative teams in film and production and is now exported to large organisations. Hypercreative is a trademark of Stormridge LLC (Sheridan, Wyoming, USA).
