Three moves. Nothing to organize.

01 Save
Drop it in

Any fragment goes in with one tap. No tags, no folders, ever. Capture has to cost almost nothing, so you never stop doing it.

02 Wait
Loam reads it

In the background, a photo goes through OCR, a voice note is transcribed, a link is summarized. You do nothing. Nothing to organise.

03 Connect
Get unstuck

Pose a real problem. Loam brings your own notes back, near and far, each turned into a question. Including something you had forgotten.

Not an answer machine. It brings your own ideas back.

Type the problem you are stuck on. Loam surfaces your own saved notes, some close to the problem and some far from it, and turns them into one question that connects them. It never writes the idea for you, so the breakthrough stays yours.

Loam does not solve it. Your own AI does.

Solving is what AI is for. So one tap builds a prompt with your related seeds already inside it, ready to paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or whatever you use. The answer comes back built from your own ideas, and you start the conversation from there, not from a blank page.
