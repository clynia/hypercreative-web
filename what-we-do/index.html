What we do

We train creativity under pressure

We teach the leadership and teams of large companies to produce useful, original work on demand, when the deadline is real and the rules keep changing.

The method we built for ourselves

This did not start as something to sell. We built it inside our own creative teams, in film and production, where the schedule is impossible, the budget runs out, the brief changes halfway and the work still has to be brilliant on time. That pressure is the closest thing there is to how a leadership team actually operates.

We turned what worked into a method, so it would survive without any single person in the room. Now we export it to the companies that need it most.

Tailor-made creativity training programs

A program designed around your team and your real challenge, from a half day to a full transformation. We train the individual mind first, then the team, then put both under real pressure with a real deliverable. Built for you, never off a shelf.

How the work runs

Everything follows three acts. The mind: train each person to focus, capture, connect and reframe. The team: turn talented individuals into one creative instrument with a shared language. The shoot: apply it to a real challenge, on the clock, with the rules changing. That last act is where it is proven.
