The 7 laws of our manifesto

We did not invent creativity. We refuse to leave it to luck.

  1. 01

    We believe creativity is the most valuable asset a company owns, and the worst trained.

  2. 02

    It was never a gift handed to a lucky few. It is a discipline, and discipline can be built.

  3. 03

    The hard part is not having ideas. It is having the useful one, on time, when everything is against you. That is the only test that matters: creativity under pressure.

  4. 04

    If it cannot be repeated, it is not a method, it is luck. What you can name, break into steps and parameterize, you can run again on demand.

  5. 05

    Every mind creates differently, so a rigid method is a broken one. Ours adapts to the person in front of it, not the other way around.

  6. 06

    A team that creates together beats a room full of talent creating apart.

  7. 07

    The future belongs to the people who can build the answer no one has yet.
